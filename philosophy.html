Sunset Philosophy

The process of watching a sunset is what makes them so special.

Pictures of a sunset are simply never as good as seeing it in person because the best part of a sunset isn't its beauty but rather the process of watching it.

Taking time out of your day to forget about your worries and simply enjoy the beauty of nature is the best part of a sunset.
