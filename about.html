About

Hello! I am Chase Carnaroli and I am from San Diego, California

Everything I know about photography is self taught. I never actually took any classes. Figuring things out as I go has always been my learning style.

Nature photography is my favorite. I like getting shots of people enjoying nature, as can be seen on my portfolio page.

When I'm not taking pictures, I am most likely out hiking, playing beach volleyball, or programming!
